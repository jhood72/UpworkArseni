5 primitive datatypes

1. Numbers = 4, 1.3, -10
2. Strings = "Hello World" or "43"
3. Booleans = true or false
4. Null or undefined

Built-In methods

    
                
  1. clear() -> simply clears the screen.
    2. 
                
  2. alert("Hi"): this would create a pop on the screen with the text "Hi".  You could also give it a number, like alert(1234); .
    3. 
                
  3. console.log("text goes here"): This sends a message to the console and NOT the screen. Good for troubleshooting bugs. 
    4. 
                
  4. prompt("text goes here"): Gives you a pop-up prompts on the screen.
    5. 
                
    A great example of how to use all three together would be:
                var userName = prompt("What is your first name");
                alert("Nice to meet you, " + userName);
                console.log("Also great to meet you, " + userName);
